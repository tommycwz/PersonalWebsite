Color Addictivity


Tag:

Game Development

Unity

C#

My game project is named Color Addictivity. My inspirations for this game are from color bump, a game where objects change colors when they collide, and the additive color table, which is a table that predicts the appearance of colors when they are mixed. The basic ideas of this game are to pass through a colored wall with 2 combined primary colors of your choice. If you hit the wall with the correct combination, you will gain 10 points. Otherwise, you will lose 3 points per wall. To make it more challenging for the player, once the player falls off the platform, you will be treated as failing immediately. There are 9 possible color combinations. For instance, if you combine green and red, you will get yellow; green and blue to get cyan; red and blue to get magenta and etc. There are also chances where you can get primary-colored walls like red. To get the color, you will need to combine 2 red colors, and this also applies to both green and blue walls. To win the level, you will have to get past the finish line at the end of each level with 70% of the total points. There are a total of 2 levels in this game.
