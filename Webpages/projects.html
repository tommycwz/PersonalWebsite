Color Addictivity

My assignment project is named as Color Addictivity. My inspirations for this game are from color bump, a game where objects change colors when they collide, and the additive color table, which is a table that predicts the appearance of colors when they mixed. The basic ideas of this game are to get pass through a colored wall with 2 combined primary color of your choice. You will need to score 70% of the total score to clear the level. If you hit the wall with the correct combination, you will gain 10 points. Otherwise, you will lose 3 points per wall. To make it more challenging for the player, once the player falls off the platform, you will be treated as failing immediately. There are 9 possible color combinations.For instance, if you combine green and red color, you will get yellow; green and blue to get cyan; red and blue to get magenta and more. There are also chances where you can get primary-colored walls like red. To get the color, you will need to combine 2 red colors, and they are also applicable to both green and blue walls. To win the level, you will have to get past the finish line at the end of each level with 70% of the total points. There is a total of 2 levels in this game.
